About Our Morning School Program

Our Morning School program is designed for students who thrive in early hours or need flexible scheduling. Running from 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM, this program offers the same quality education as our day school with condensed but comprehensive learning sessions.

Ideal for students who work part-time, have other commitments in the afternoon, or simply prefer morning learning. Our experienced faculty ensures effective knowledge transfer in optimized time slots with focus on examination preparation.

Flexible Timing

Morning hours allowing afternoon freedom for other activities
