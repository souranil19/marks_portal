About Muraripukur Government Sponsored Higher Secondary School

Established in 1985, Muraripukur Government Sponsored Higher Secondary School has been a beacon of educational excellence in West Bengal. We blend traditional Indian values with modern pedagogical approaches to create a nurturing environment where every child can thrive.

Our commitment to holistic education ensures that students not only excel academically but also develop strong character, leadership skills, and cultural awareness.
