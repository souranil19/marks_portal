About Our Day School Program

Our Day School program offers a comprehensive educational experience from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, providing extended learning opportunities for students from Class VI to XII. With modern infrastructure, expert faculty, and a focus on both academics and co-curricular activities.

Students benefit from smaller class sizes, personalized attention, and access to state-of-the-art facilities throughout the day. Our program emphasizes practical learning, critical thinking, and character development alongside academic excellence.
