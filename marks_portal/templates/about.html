About Muraripukur Government Sponsored Higher Secondary School

Discover our journey of educational excellence spanning over two decades of nurturing young minds and building tomorrow's leaders.

Our Story

Education is the complete development of the perfection already present within human beings. In the development of well-equipped human resources, education is the best means. Only through education can the dormant powers within human beings be awakened to overcome physical, mental, and spiritual limitations.

In the language of the scriptures: “Education is the manifestation of the perfection already in man.”

Swami Vivekananda said, “The purpose of education is man-making. It is man-making education alone that we want.”

The primary goal of education is to help students become complete human beings. Therefore, education must be contemporary and inclusive. Through education, students can gain a clear understanding of the world by training the mind with a broad, integrated, and harmonious outlook.

Dr. S. Radhakrishnan said: “The purpose of Education is to provide a coherent picture of the universe and an integrated way of life, we must obtain through it a sense of perspective synoptic vision. 'Saman Vaya' (Harmony), of the different items of knowledge.”

Modern educators aim to develop the personality and social awareness of students through education. Therefore, alongside academic learning, students should also acquire moral values. According to Rabindranath Tagore, education must include freedom of mind, freedom of heart, and spiritual freedom.

With this objective, Rabindra Vidyamandir was founded in the industrial city of Kolkata, Jute Town (now known as Kankinara), with the initiative of the Indian Iron and Steel Company (IISCO) under the chairmanship of Dr. Nirmal Kumar Mukherjee. On December 20, 1965, the Junior Section (from Class I to Class V) was established and inaugurated by the then Education Minister, Rabindralal Singh Mahasay.

This institution has developed in the heart of the historically significant Murari Pukur Baganbari area of Bagmari, a place closely associated with the independence struggle and the secret revolutionary organization led by Khudiram Bose.

Originally a center of weaving and technology, this historic institution was upgraded to a higher secondary school in 1976. In 1977, the curriculum expanded to include Arts and Science streams. Following the education policy of 1976–77, Commerce and Vocational streams were also introduced.

These streams continue today. With approval from the West Bengal Council of Vocational Education and Training, students receive training in job-oriented courses from Class IX onwards. This initiative has opened new avenues for students in North and East Kolkata, enabling many to receive professional training starting from school.

This Vocational Education & Training plays a vital role in the social and economic development of students.

Rabindra Vidyamandir, a Bengali medium school in Kolkata, has become a symbol of mental discipline and excellence. Many modern schools now take inspiration from this model.

Those who are committed to the ideals of Murari Pukur and passionate about education will stay with us — others will not. With the legacy of the past and new plans for the future, this school continues to move forward.

Our Educational Philosophy

Holistic Development: We believe in nurturing the intellectual, emotional, physical, and spiritual growth of every student.

Personalized Learning: We recognize that each child is unique and adapt our teaching methods to suit individual learning styles.

Global Perspective: We prepare students to be global citizens while staying rooted in Indian values and culture.

Community Partnership: We believe in strong collaboration between school, family, and community for student success.

Why Choose Muraripukur?

At Muraripukur Government Sponsored Higher Secondary School, we don't just teach subjects; we shape futures. Our comprehensive approach to education ensures that students develop critical thinking skills, emotional intelligence, and a strong moral compass.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and innovative teaching methods create an environment where students can discover their passions, develop their talents, and prepare for success in an ever-changing world.

We are proud of our track record of academic excellence, with 95% of our students achieving their first-choice university admissions and many receiving prestigious scholarships and awards.
